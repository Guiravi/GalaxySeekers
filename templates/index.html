About The Tool

This visualization tool uses data from NASA's High Energy Astrophysics Science Archive Research Center (HEASARCH). It uses images from various telescope to display what the sky looks in different wavelenghts of light. Also, it displays where these telescopes pointed at on the most recent observations or any other given date given by the user.
